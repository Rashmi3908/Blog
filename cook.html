double chocolate and caramel trifle

Why to cook? Cooking or cookery is the art, technology, science and craft of preparing food for consumption with or without the use of fire or heat.

Recipe:

for double chocolate and caramel trifle

Brownies, chopped into pieces
1 jar Caramel Sauce
1 jar Hot Fudge Sauce
1 cup heavy whipping cream
½ cup powdered sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1 cup toasted almonds.

Whip heavy cream in bowl.Whisk until soft peaks form.Add powdered sugar and vanilla. Continue to whisk until whipped cream is firm, until desired consistency. keep in freeze. Add ¼ cup brownie bites to each trifle dish. Drizzle 1 tablespoon (or more if the mood strikes you) hot fudge sauce over brownie. Dollop 2 tablespoons vanilla cream over top. Add 2 tablespoons brownies and drizzle 1 tablespoon (or more if the mood strikes you) caramel sauce over brownie. Repeat until all ingredients are used.
